About This Project
Dataset
5,000 smart meter readings at 30-minute intervals across 3 dorms and 24 rooms. Features include temperature, humidity, wind speed, and historical consumption, normalized to a 0–1 scale.
Model
{{ stats.model_type }} trained on 13 features including Dorm ID and Room ID. Time-based 70/30 train-test split preserves temporal integrity.
Feature Engineering
IsWeekend, Season (0–3), and TimeOfDay are auto-derived from time inputs. Dorm and Room IDs are label-encoded (Dorm A=0, B=1, C=2; Room 101–108 → 0–7).
Anomaly Detection
Rule-based: predictions above 0.75 are flagged as High Consumption. Status is shown on every prediction result and in the history log.
